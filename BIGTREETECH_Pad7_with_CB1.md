# BIGTREETECH Pad 7 with BIGTREETECH CB1

With the stock Armbian, [LAN and sound do not work](https://forum.armbian.com/topic/58747-pad-7-with-cb1-no-lan-and-no-sound/).

Bigtreetech provides an [Armbian fork](https://github.com/bigtreetech/build) that enables both LAN and sound.

The speakers aren't the best, but they are totally suitable for [simple signaling or speech prompts](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K5x3_V_F5kk).

The screen quality is decent. The whole device is a bit too thick and there's plenty of free space inside, but no headers to attach any additional equipment. An internal USB port would be great.
